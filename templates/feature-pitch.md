# Feature Pitch / 功能提案

Use for proposing new features, budget requests, roadmap prioritization, or stakeholder buy-in.

## English Version

**Feature:** [Name]
**Prepared for:** [Audience]
**Decision Needed:** [Approve / Prioritize / Fund / Review]

### Executive Summary

[1-3 sentences: customer problem, proposed solution, expected business value, and requested decision.]

### Problem

[Who experiences the problem, how often, and why it matters to the business.]

### Evidence

| Evidence | Source | Business Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| [Metric, customer quote, market signal] | [Source] | [Meaning] |

### Proposed Solution

[Plain-language feature description. Avoid implementation detail unless it affects scope, cost, or risk.]

### Benefits

| Benefit | Expected Impact | Confidence |
|---|---|---|
| [Benefit] | [Impact] | [High / Medium / Low] |

### Investment

| Resource | Estimate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| People | [Estimate] | [Notes] |
| Time | [Estimate] | [Notes] |
| Budget | [Estimate] | [Notes] |

### Risks and Mitigation

| Risk | Business Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| [Risk] | [Impact] | [Mitigation] |

### Recommendation

[Decision request, preferred timeline, and success metric.]

## Traditional Chinese Version

**功能：** [名稱]
**對象：** [受眾]
**需要的決策：** [核准 / 排入優先順序 / 提供預算 / 審閱]

### 摘要

[1-3 句：客戶問題、建議方案、預期商業價值、需要的決策。]

### 問題

[誰遇到這個問題、發生頻率、為什麼對業務重要。]

### 證據

| 證據 | 來源 | 商業意義 |
|---|---|---|
| [指標、客戶回饋、市場訊號] | [來源] | [意義] |

### 建議方案

[用白話描述功能。除非會影響範圍、成本或風險，否則避免實作細節。]

### 效益

| 效益 | 預期影響 | 信心程度 |
|---|---|---|
| [效益] | [影響] | [高 / 中 / 低] |

### 投資需求

| 資源 | 估算 | 備註 |
|---|---|---|
| 人力 | [估算] | [備註] |
| 時間 | [估算] | [備註] |
| 預算 | [估算] | [備註] |

### 風險與緩解

| 風險 | 商業影響 | 緩解方式 |
|---|---|---|
| [風險] | [影響] | [緩解方式] |

### 建議

[決策請求、建議時程與成功指標。]
