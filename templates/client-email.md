# Client Email / 客戶信件

Use for customer-facing updates about incidents, delays, security notices, maintenance, or product changes.

## English Version

**Subject:** [Clear subject]

Hi [Name/Team],

[Opening sentence: what happened or what is changing.]

**Current status:** [Resolved / In progress / Scheduled / Under review]

**Customer impact:** [What the customer experienced or whether they are affected.]

**What we are doing:** [Actions taken or planned.]

**Action required from you:** [None / Specific action]

**Next update:** [Time or condition for next update]

Thank you,

[Team/Name]

## Traditional Chinese Version

**主旨：** [清楚主旨]

[姓名/團隊] 您好：

[開場：發生了什麼，或即將有什麼變更。]

**目前狀態：** [已解決 / 處理中 / 已排程 / 審查中]

**客戶影響：** [客戶遇到的狀況，或是否受到影響。]

**我們正在做的事：** [已採取或預計採取的行動。]

**需要您採取的行動：** [無 / 具體行動]

**下一次更新：** [時間或條件]

謝謝，

[團隊/姓名]
