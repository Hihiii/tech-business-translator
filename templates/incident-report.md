# Incident Report / 事故報告

Use for active or resolved service interruptions, degraded performance, data incidents, or security incidents.

## English Version

**Service/System:** [Name]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Severity:** [Critical / High / Medium / Low]
**Status:** [Investigating / Mitigating / Resolved / Closed]
**Audience:** [Executive / VP / PM / Client / Legal / Internal]

### Executive Summary

[1-3 sentences: what happened, who was affected, business impact, and current status.]

### Customer and Business Impact

| Area | Detail |
|---|---|
| Affected users | [Number, percentage, segment, or "unknown"] |
| Duration | [Start time, end time, total duration] |
| User experience | [What users saw or could not do] |
| Revenue / SLA / compliance impact | [Known impact or "not yet quantified"] |
| Support impact | [Ticket volume, escalation count, or "none known"] |

### Timeline

| Time | Event | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|
| [HH:MM timezone] | [Detection] | [What changed for users or operations] |
| [HH:MM timezone] | [Response] | [How impact was contained] |
| [HH:MM timezone] | [Resolution] | [Service restored or risk reduced] |

### What Happened

[Plain-language cause and effect. Explain technical details only if needed for trust or accountability.]

### Resolution

[What was done, what is working now, and what remains under observation.]

### Prevention Plan

| Action | Owner | Target Date | Success Measure |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Action] | [Team/Person] | [Date] | [Measurable outcome] |

### Open Questions

- [Unknown that affects impact, risk, or next decision]

## Traditional Chinese Version

**服務/系統：** [名稱]
**日期：** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**嚴重程度：** [重大 / 高 / 中 / 低]
**狀態：** [調查中 / 緩解中 / 已解決 / 已結案]
**對象：** [高階主管 / VP / PM / 客戶 / 法務 / 內部團隊]

### 摘要

[1-3 句：發生了什麼、誰受到影響、商業影響、目前狀態。]

### 用戶與商業影響

| 項目 | 說明 |
|---|---|
| 受影響用戶 | [數量、比例、客群，或「尚未確認」] |
| 影響時間 | [開始時間、結束時間、總時長] |
| 用戶體驗 | [用戶看到什麼，或無法完成什麼] |
| 營收 / SLA / 合規影響 | [已知影響，或「尚未量化」] |
| 客服影響 | [工單量、升級處理數，或「目前未發現」] |

### 時間軸

| 時間 | 事件 | 商業影響 |
|---|---|---|

| [HH:MM 時區] | [恢復服務] | [服務恢復或風險降低] |

### 事件說明

[用白話說明原因與影響鏈。只有在建立信任或交代責任需要時才加入技術細節。]

### 解決狀態

[已採取的修復、目前恢復狀態、仍在觀察的項目。]
