# Traditional Chinese Style Guide

Use professional Taiwan-facing Traditional Chinese. Translate business meaning rather than English sentence structure.

## Preferred Terms

| English | Preferred Traditional Chinese |
|---|---|
| business impact | 商業影響 |
| customer impact | 客戶影響 |
| user impact | 用戶影響 |
| risk mitigation | 風險緩解、風險控管 |
| current status | 目前狀態 |
| next steps | 後續行動 |
| decision needed | 待決策事項 |
| recommendation | 建議 |
| estimated timeline | 預估時程 |
| confirmed facts | 已確認事實 |
| open questions | 待確認事項 |
| customer action required | 客戶需採取的行動 |
| no action required | 客戶目前不需採取行動 |

## Style Rules

- Prefer concise business prose over literal translation.
- Use "目前" when facts may change.
- Use "依目前證據判斷" for investigation updates.
- Use "尚未量化" when business impact is unknown.
- Avoid colloquial filler such as "基本上", "大概", "應該沒事".
- Avoid over-softening serious risks.

## Client-facing Phrases

- "目前服務已恢復，團隊仍持續監控。"
- "目前沒有證據顯示客戶資料受到影響。"
- "除非另行通知，客戶目前不需要採取行動。"
- "我們會在確認更多資訊後提供下一次更新。"

## Executive-facing Phrases

- "此事件的主要商業影響為..."
- "目前最重要的決策事項是..."
- "建議優先投入資源處理..."
- "若不處理，未來 3-6 個月的風險包含..."

## Legal-sensitive Phrases

- "目前證據顯示..."
- "影響範圍仍在確認中。"
- "此說明應在對外發布前交由法務審閱。"
- "目前尚未確認是否涉及合規通報義務。"
