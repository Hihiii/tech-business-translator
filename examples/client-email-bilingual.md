# Example: Bilingual Client Email

## Prompt

Write a bilingual client email:

> Scheduled maintenance: database migration on June 10 02:00-03:00 UTC. Read-only mode for admin reports. No customer-facing checkout impact expected.

## Expected Traits

- Customer-facing and concise.
- Preserve date, time window, read-only admin reports, and no expected checkout impact.
- Include action required.

## English Sample

Subject: Scheduled maintenance for admin reports on June 10

Hi team,

We will perform scheduled maintenance on June 10 from 02:00 to 03:00 UTC. During this window, admin reports will be temporarily read-only. We do not expect any impact to customer-facing checkout.

Action required: No action is required from your team.

## Traditional Chinese Sample

主旨：6 月 10 日管理報表排程維護通知

您好：

我們將於 6 月 10 日 02:00 至 03:00 UTC 進行排程維護。在此期間，管理報表會暫時進入唯讀模式。預期不會影響客戶端結帳流程。

需要您採取的行動：貴團隊目前不需要採取任何行動。
