# Example: Bilingual Project Plan

## Prompt

Plan and delegate a project:

> Build a client-facing incident communication package from engineering logs, customer tickets, and a draft RCA. Need English and Traditional Chinese outputs. Include QA and legal-safe review.

## Expected Traits

- Route to project plan and stakeholder incident pack.
- Include workstreams for log review, customer impact, RCA translation, legal-safe review, bilingual consistency, and final QA.
- Preserve unknowns and validation steps.

## English Sample

The project should be split into six workstreams: evidence review, customer impact assessment, RCA translation, legal-safe messaging, bilingual consistency review, and final delivery QA. Each workstream needs a clear output and validation method before the final communication package is assembled.

## Traditional Chinese Sample

此專案應拆成六個工作流：證據檢視、客戶影響評估、RCA 商業化轉譯、法務安全措辭、雙語一致性檢查，以及最終交付 QA。每個工作流都需要明確產出與驗證方式，才能整合成最終溝通包。
