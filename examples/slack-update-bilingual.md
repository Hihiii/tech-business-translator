# Example: Bilingual Slack Update

## Prompt

Write a short bilingual Slack update:

> Payment provider timeout increased. Checkout success down 4%. Team switched traffic to backup provider. Monitoring for 30 min.

## Expected Traits

- Short and operational.
- Preserve 4% checkout impact and 30-minute monitoring.
- State action and owner if known.

## English Sample

**Status:** Mitigated, monitoring

- **Impact:** Checkout success dropped by 4% during payment provider timeouts.
- **Action:** Traffic has been switched to the backup provider.
- **Next update:** After 30 minutes of monitoring, or sooner if success rate drops again.

## Traditional Chinese Sample

**狀態：** 已緩解，監控中

- **影響：** 付款供應商逾時期間，結帳成功率下降 4%。
- **行動：** 流量已切換至備援付款供應商。
- **下一次更新：** 監控 30 分鐘後更新；若成功率再次下降會提前通知。
