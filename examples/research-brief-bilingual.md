# Example: Bilingual Research Brief

## Prompt

Research a set of legal and financial documents and produce a bilingual review-ready brief.

## Expected Traits

- Route to unattended research guide and research brief template.
- Include source inventory, evidence table, confidence labels, caveats, and next steps.
- Avoid legal or investment advice.

## English Sample

The research deliverable should identify the question, scope, source inventory, key findings, confidence level, and caveats. Legal and financial interpretations should be framed as research observations, not professional advice.

## Traditional Chinese Sample

研究交付物應明確列出研究問題、範圍、來源清單、主要發現、信心程度與限制。法律與金融解讀應定位為研究觀察，而不是專業法律或投資建議。
