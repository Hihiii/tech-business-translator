# Example: Bilingual Decision Memo

## Prompt

Create a bilingual decision memo:

> Option A: increase infra budget $3k/month to reduce latency 40%. Option B: defer and accept current p95 1.8s. Checkout conversion likely impacted but exact revenue not quantified.

## Expected Traits

- Preserve $3k/month, 40%, p95 1.8s, unquantified revenue.
- Recommend only if justified.
- Label unknown revenue impact.

## English Sample

Recommendation: choose Option A if checkout speed is a near-term priority, because it reduces response time risk with a known monthly cost. The financial upside is not yet quantified, so the decision should be framed as risk reduction and customer experience improvement rather than confirmed ROI.

## Traditional Chinese Sample

建議：若近期優先目標是改善結帳速度，建議採用選項 A，因為它以已知的每月 3,000 美元成本降低回應時間風險。由於財務效益尚未量化，此決策應定位為降低風險與改善客戶體驗，而不是已確認的 ROI。
