# Example: Bilingual Feature Pitch

## Prompt

Create a bilingual feature pitch:

> Add dark mode. 40% of support tickets mention eye strain. Competitor X shipped it last quarter. Engineering estimates 2 weeks for one senior frontend dev.

## Expected Traits

- Audience: product leadership.
- Template: feature pitch.
- Preserve support ticket percentage, competitor signal, and effort estimate.
- Explain value as customer satisfaction and competitive parity.

## English Sample

Dark mode addresses a clear customer comfort issue and closes a competitive gap. Forty percent of related support tickets mention eye strain, and a direct competitor shipped this capability last quarter. The estimated investment is two weeks for one senior user-interface engineer.

Recommendation: prioritize dark mode as a small, high-signal customer satisfaction improvement.

## Traditional Chinese Sample

深色模式可解決明確的客戶使用舒適度問題，也能補上競品差距。相關客服工單中有 40% 提到眼睛疲勞，而直接競品已在上一季推出此功能。預估投入為一位資深前端工程師兩週。

建議：將深色模式列為小範圍但高訊號的客戶滿意度改善項目。
